Crystal Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There will be four crystals and clicking each one will add a specific amount to your total score. You win by matching your total score to the random number given. If you go over you lose. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it and each game the values of the crystals will change.
